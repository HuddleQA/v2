Lesson review tool
What this tool does

The Huddle QA tool reviews your lesson production doc before filming and gives you a one-page report — what's working, what to fix, and a traffic-light verdict.

How it works
1
Open the Huddle QA Project in Claude
Go to claude.ai, open your Projects, and click Huddle QA. Start a new chat.
2
Upload your LP doc
Drag and drop your lesson production doc (PDF or Word) into the chat, or paste the text in directly. Claude will review it immediately — no need to type anything.
3
Read your report in chat
Your full QA report appears directly in the chat — verdict, scores, strengths, and fixes. This is your feedback. Read it here.
4
Save to Drive
At the bottom of the Claude report there's a JSON block. Copy it and come back here to save a formatted copy to the shared Huddle QA Reports folder.
💾 Go to save report →
Good to know
📋  Review each lesson in a fresh chat — don't review two lessons in the same conversation.
💬  The report is your feedback. You don't need to save to Drive to read it — saving is just so Alexa has a record.
🔧  If something was flagged and you want to fix it, take it to your lesson creation chat, not the QA chat.
❓  Something not working? Message Alexa.
